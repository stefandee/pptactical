The 0.9.0 RC1 version will undertake a stress test during February 2003. The bugs that will be found will be fixed and further RC’s will be available for download.

The next version is 0.9.5. There is not a target date for this release, since it is unclear of the features it will include. However, here’s a brief wishlist:

  • Aircrafts – fighters (fixed and rotary wing) and dropships
  • Layered buildings (animated buildings)
  • Save and load
  • Png loading support
  • Alphablending
  • Battlegroup scripting
